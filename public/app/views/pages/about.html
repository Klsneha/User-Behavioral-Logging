Why did I decide to log these Actions:

When a User refers a stack over flow page they observe what are the possible answers given by users. while going through the page the most common behaviors performed are:

  • UpVote and DownVote: When a User reads a solution and find it to be a apt solution they tend to upvote it, similarly when they dont like the solution they downvote it. These feedbacks will be helpful for the rest of the users.

  • Add a Comment: A highly active stack over flow user if knows solution for a any particular question, tries to attempt it by adding a comment.

  • Ask a Question: A User who is unable to figure out a solution will ask a question on stack over flow website. This can be done by clicking on the Ask a question button.

  • Related Question: Stack overflow also displayes related questions on the lft side of the page. If a user find them intresting they can refer them.

Analysis and Patterns

  • According to the visualization most of the users are giving feedback by clicking on upvote and downvote. 30% of all the user behavior actions are upvote and downvote.

  • All the users after exploring the page are preferring to navigate to related questions to seek further guidance. 60% of all behavior action is clicking on related Questions.

  • Only few users take an interest to actively participate in discussing a solution by posting comments. 10% of all the behavior actions is Add a Comment.

Explaining the Patterns

  • According to the observations, most of the users prefer reading the solutions provided and look for a better solution by looking into more related questions.

Steps to Finding the Results

  • By observing the statistic around 60% of the users prefer Reading the solution and click on related Question.
